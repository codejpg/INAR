I'm not a robot

The interactive experience „I’m not a robot“ allows users to connect to an alternative timeline through a phone. Created as a space for social exchange it’s now a place where useless AI-Systems and irrelevant cultural artifacts roam without any purpose.
You can visit this experience during the international film festival Sehsüchte in Waschhaus Potsdam from 21. April 2023 until 23. April 2023 Schiffbauergasse 6, 14467 Potsdam.
